Wear a Helmet Why?

It is estimated that every year, more than 400,000 motorcycle riders and passengers are killed or seriously injured in road crashes. Wearing a helmet is essential for motorcycle riders and passengers to protect their head and brain from injury in case of an accident. The helmet reduces the impact of a crash on the head and can save lives. Studies have shown that helmets are around 37% effective in preventing motorcycle deaths and around 67% effective in preventing brain injuries. Therefore, it is crucial for motorcycle riders and passengers to wear a helmet while riding to reduce the risk of severe injury or death in case of an accident.

What is the importance of This project

Developing a machine learning computer vision project that can identify whether a motorcycle rider is wearing a helmet or not is important for several reasons.
Firstly, it can assist law enforcement agencies in enforcing helmet laws and ensuring rider safety. In many countries, wearing a helmet while riding a motorcycle is mandatory, and non-compliance can result in hefty fines. However, enforcing this law can be challenging, especially in areas with high traffic volumes. A computer vision system can be used to monitor and identify helmet usage, helping authorities enforce helmet laws more effectively.
Secondly, the system can also help insurance companies to offer customized insurance policies based on a rider's safety record. Insurers can use data generated by the system to assess the risk profile of a rider and tailor their policies accordingly. Riders who consistently wear helmets while riding are less likely to be involved in accidents and suffer injuries, and thus, they may be eligible for lower premiums.
Finally, the system can also raise awareness about the importance of wearing a helmet while riding a motorcycle. In many cases, riders may be unaware of the risks associated with not wearing a helmet, or they may choose not to wear one due to various reasons. By detecting helmet usage, the system can help educate riders on the importance of wearing a helmet and encourage them to adopt safe riding practices.
Overall, developing a machine learning computer vision project to detect helmet usage is crucial for promoting rider safety and reducing the number of motorcycle-related injuries and fatalities.
